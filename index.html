Picture Perfected

Pushing the envelope of innovation.

Our 4K Ultra HD TV isn’t just 4 times clearer than Full HD, with upscaling for 1080p content to Ultra HD, it also enhances your picture with groundbreaking technologies to deliver a viewing experience like you’ve never experienced.

Revolutionary Color & High Dynamic RangeFor the first time, High Dynamic Range (HDR) imaging brings color volume to your screens that is multiple times richer than other TVs. With Ultra Color Spectrum Technology and revolutionary 800 nit LED backlighting, Reference Series renders true 10-bit color that is closer to the human eye than ever before seen on a TV Screen.

800 nits LED Backlight

Contrast Beyond CompareReference Series’ incredible precision contrast is enabled by the backlighting of 384 Active LED Zones and enhanced with intelligent Active Pixel Tuning technology that adjusts each pixel for richness and detail that makes images jump off the screen.

384Active LED Zones

Motion Smoothness RedefinedAn unparalleled 240 Motion Clarity Rate (MCR) is measured by a whole new standard that includes ultra-high refresh rates, a dedicated Ultra HD VM50 engine and scanning backlight, enriched with motion enhancement/motion compensation features that eliminate judder and blurring.

240Motion Clarity Rate

Authentic Entertainment FidelityRevolutionary High Velocity Mode and a Pure Cinema Engine adjust frame rates to faithfully deliver movies and games as their makers meant them to be experienced, presenting films in their native cinematic 24 or 48 fps, and games at 120 fps – unlike other TVs that convert the image to adapt to a 3:2 pulldown frame rate.
